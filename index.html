Mathematical Synthesizer

Hi welcome to Harmasay! This tool converts two mathematical equations, including complex functions, into musical tones simultaneously. Enter two equations, click "Play," and listen to the combined sound of mathematics!

Supported Equation Types:

  • Real and complex functions
  • Polynomial functions: x^2, 2x + 1, x^3 - 2x + 3
  • Trigonometric functions: sin(x), cos(x), tan(x)
  • Exponential and logarithmic: e^x, ln(x), log(x)
  • Complex operations: 1/(1-x), e^(i*x), sqrt(x)
  • Constants: pi, e, i (imaginary unit)
  • Basic arithmetic: +, -, *, /
  • Mathematical functions: abs(x), arg(x), re(x), im(x)

Note: The synthesizer evaluates the equations for x values between -10 and 10.

Warning: Please make sure to reduce the volume!
